Design that feels effortless because I did the hard part

A UX/UI designer with 4 years of experience across products, industries, and freelance work. I've worked on projects ranging from structured product environments to fast-paced freelance — which taught me to adapt quickly, think practically, and design for real-world constraints. I've handled end-to-end design journeys: from shaping vague ideas and structuring flows to creating wireframes, building UI, and collaborating with developers. Freelancing especially helped me take ownership, communicate clearly, and design solutions that truly solve user and business problems. What defines my approach is simple — I simplify before I design, question before I assume, and prioritize usability over unnecessary complexity. I turn messy ideas into intuitive experiences, while constantly exploring and learning. I design quietly opinionated products that respect a user's time, attention, and intent.
